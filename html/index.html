Philosophy

We offer our consultants free training, pay them a fair wage, and encourage them to move on.

Impact sourcing, also known as socially responsible outsourcing, refers to an arm of the business process outsourcing (BPO) industry that employs people at the base of the pyramid as workers. It is defined as employing socioeconomically disadvantaged individuals as principle workers in business process outsourcing centers to provide high-quality, information-based services to domestic and international clients. (wikipedia)
