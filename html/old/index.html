High Quality Impact Sourcing

Impact sourcing, also known as socially responsible outsourcing, refers to an arm of the business process outsourcing (BPO) industry that employs people at the base of the pyramid as workers. It is defined as employing socioeconomically disadvantaged individuals as principle workers in business process outsourcing centers to provide high-quality, information-based services to domestic and international clients. (wikipedia)

At Upsource, we work our consultants through a rigorous training program that encompasses technology and business material. As a result, we deliver thoughtful, timely, high-quality work with professionalism.

Our goal is to enable our consultants to provide for their families and empower them with the training, skills, and tools they need to advance and thrive in the modern world, ultimately moving on from Upsource if they choose.
