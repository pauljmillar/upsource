The Booth

Our photobooth uses professional equipment including; a high megapixel camera, softbox lighting system and a printer that laminates the photos at the same time as the printing process, so the photos come out dry and smudge resistant (remember going to the mall and grabbing your pics out of the old school photobooth and ruining them with your fingerprints?...not here!!). Our printer is super fast-the prints are ready in less than one minute!

Modern black on black baroque pattern decorates the exterior of our photobooth to complement the décor of your event. The interior of the photobooth boasts a sleek black design and a variety of backdrops.

4squareFOTO photobooth is 7 feet tall and has a footprint of 5 feet by 6 feet. Make sure to leave extra room around the booth for your guests………4squareFOTO photobooths always draw a crowd!! Although our photobooth takes up very little space, it is large enough to fit 4 adults sitting comfortably, or even more getting friendly:)
